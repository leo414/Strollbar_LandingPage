Search for for photos

Use the map-view to detect photos around you. A red ring will appear around the inner circle to let you know you are within range of viewable photos. A yellow ring will appear around the outer circle to let you know you are within range of photos which are not viewable from you current position but will be viewable once you get closer.

View found photos

When a red ring appears around the inner circle, swipe the bottom slider up to see what’s available.

Historical Preservationist: When you take a photo, it’s as if you’re freezing time in any given location and allowing whatever you captured to be viewed by people who go there in the future. We never know how places might change over time, but with Strollbar, you play a direct role in preserving the life of a place in photographic form. So when people pass by one day, they can say “Wow, that happened right here, years ago!” They’ll have you to thank.
